Junior Suite

Junior suites are perfect for travelers, who just like to taste the feeling of luxury hotel suites. If you have never ever booked a hotel suite before, may you try junior suite first.

Executive Suite

If you are a business person, you probably have scheduled meetings or you need a calm and quiet place where you can deal with your business matters. Executive suite is perfect for business men and women, and top managers of companies because these suites are combining together a fully serviced office, meeting room and a luxurious hotel room together.

Terrace Suite

If you like big terraces with wonderful view and you like to spend most of your time outside instead of inside in your vacation, terrace suite will be perfect for you. Terrace suite is the most popular and the most beloved suite type. Why? Simply because it offers a large outdoor terrace with garden furniture, while offering spacious luxury interior as well. Depending on the weather, you can decide where you want to spend the time when you are in the hotel.

Deluxe Suite

A classical minimalist style suite with high quality materials. Deluxe suites are recommended for people who like spacious accommodation in reasonable price. In luxury hospitality Deluxe suite is the first “true suite” in the suite types list, because it offers separated living area and sleeping area and meet all of the luxury suite requirements. Some of these suites have balcony as well, however this usually specified in the suite name.

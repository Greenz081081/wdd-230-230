Visual Hierarchy

Narock Site

nacorock.com

Just as Visual Hierarchy refers to the arranging of components in ascending or descending order of importance. The Narock site arranges its components in a descending order which provides a focal points of view for critical informations.

Proximity

Youtube Site

youtube.com

With the Youtube site, we see the design principle of proximity as the elements on the page are arranged closer together which creates connections between each of the components available on the page.

Allignment

Facebook News Site

about fb.com

As alignment provides a sense of direction, unity, and organization on a page. On the facebook news page, the page employs the design principle of alignment on the typography of the page which provides direction, and organization on the page.

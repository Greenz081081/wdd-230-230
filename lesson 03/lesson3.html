Site Name

The site name would be called Chamber Of Commerce

Purpose Statement

The main purpose of the Echamberofcommerce website will be to strengthen the business interests of business groups. The intent is to foster business networking and to advocate on the behalf of the community and, for our purposes, advertise the attractions of the city.

The other purpose of this website is to build communities (regions/states/nations) to which residents, visitors and investors are attracted; promoting those communities. Striving to ensure future prosperity via a pro-business climate; representing the unified voice of the employer community; and Reducing transactional friction through well-functioning networks.
